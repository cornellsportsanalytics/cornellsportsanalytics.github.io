About Us

We are new student organization on campus that aims to bring together students that are passionate about the field of sports analytics. We offer both a casual and professional environment for members to develop skills in statistical analysis, data visualization, and sports research.

“The insatiable thirst for a competitive edge, coupled with new technologies and advanced computing power is driving the evolution of sports from the analog to the digital era.”
