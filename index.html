About Us

We are new student organization on campus that aims to bring together students that are passionate about the field of sports analytics. We offer both a casual and professional environment for members to develop skills in statistical analysis, data visualization, and sports research.

Our Team

Cornell Sports Analytics was started by a group of die-hard sports fans who also appreciate the insights gleaned from sports analytics. With no current sports analytics clubs and inspired by sites such as FiveThirtyEight, this club was founded at the end of 2019.

  • Quality Reports
  • Teamwork
  • Innovative Research
  • Advanced Analytics

Members discuss the current state of advanced statistics in a variety of sports and attempt to contribute to the field through original projects and research.

Our Projects

Besides group-driven projects, we aim to be accepting and allow members to pursue their own projects of interest. Some examples of cool projects include:

  • Creating a March Madness model to enter in a Kaggle competition
  • Exploring the Houston Astros cheating scandal in the MLB
  • Taking a closer look at true interception rate in the NFL
  • Predicting Roger Federer's chance of winning the ATP Tour
